Admin Panel, 'Garden'

'The Garden' is an in-house admin-panel built from scratch and is still actively improving.

It controls all the contents on our front-end website, process orders, CRMs, and other departments' features, including marketing and finance teams.

In 2021, around 300 people from Trenbe and partners use our platform to process about 2000 orders daily.

My Role

I played a dual role as both the product owner and the UX designer and worked with two other developers from concepts to launch. I was responsible for creating and handing over designs and product ownership, making Trenbers' job easier and getting more things done.

I conducted stakeholder interviews, research and helping people to use new features from day one.
